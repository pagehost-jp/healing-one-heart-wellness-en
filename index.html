In 1996, I moved to the United States to start a new business.

The pressure and irregular lifestyle began to take a toll. I felt constantly tired, never quite well.

"Is this how I want to live?"
That question became the start of my learning journey.

Studying holistic nutrition showed me that it's not just about food—our relationships, our lifestyle, our way of being all affect our mind and body.

As I transformed, I wanted to support others going through similar struggles. That's when I chose to become a health coach.
